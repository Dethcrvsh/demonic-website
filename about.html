About Concerts Contacts

Demonic Science is a melodic death metal band hailing from Linköping Sweden. Drawing insperation from the old-school, ca 90-2000s, swedish melodeath scene we combine fast and melancholic riffing with angry, misanthropic lyrics about society, humanity and whatever else we feel like. With a strong DIY ethos we intend to write, mix, record and release music independant from any publishers or meddling middlemen. We do things our own way.
